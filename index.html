About Us

Why choose us?

Welcome to Rondé Construction and Design, your trusted partner in bringing your renovation dreams to life. With over 17 years of experience serving the GTA, we specialize in providing high-quality general contracting services for commercial and residential projects of all sizes. From office remodels to kitchen and bathroom renovations along with complete home renovations, our dedicated team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering exceptional craftsmanship, attention to detail, and personalized service to every client.

At Rondé we pride ourselves on our reputation for integrity, reliability, and superior results. Let us turn your vision into reality and make your office space or home the envy of the neighborhood..
